## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
A vintage-style embroidered artwork depicting three wooden shelves against a cream-colored background. The top shelf features four decorative vases - two black, one orange, and one green - each containing orange sunflowers with green stems and leaves. The middle shelf displays a collection of vintage books with weathered covers in various colors including pink, brown, and yellow, alongside a green leaf-shaped vase. The bottom shelf shows a black cat with a pink nose and white whiskers sitting among a collection of books in teal, orange, and brown colors. The books have decorative patterns and text on their spines. The entire piece has a textured, hand-stitched appearance with visible embroidery stitches in a cross-stitch style. The artwork has a cozy, literary theme with a mid-century modern aesthetic. --v 7
```
