## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
Elegant watercolor painting of Savannakhet, Laos, featuring Catholic Church, Dinosaur Museum, and That Ing Hang Stupa. Soft irregular edges blending into white background. Delicate brushstrokes, pastel color palette with blues, soft oranges, and gentle earth tones. Fine architectural details with artistic loose painting style. The illustration should have ample white space around the painted area, creating a floating effect. No text or borders. Professional travel art print style similar to gallery watercolors. --ar 3:4 --style raw --v 7
```
