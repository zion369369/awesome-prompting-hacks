## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
A sophisticated high-fashion model wearing an avant-garde tuxedo-inspired couture look: sleek black tailored tuxedo pants, a sculptural asymmetrical tuxedo top with a crisp white shirt on one side and a dramatic black lapel that crosses diagonally over the chest, fastening elegantly at the side waist. She stands on the grand staircase of the MET Opera in New York, confidently holding the diamond-studded leash of a colossal Dalmatian dog. The Dalmatian is towering over the model, massive and majestic, with a sparkling diamond collar. The contrast between the model’s black-and-white ensemble and the Dalmatian’s spotted coat creates a striking visual. Shot in rich monochrome, cinematic fashion editorial, full-body shot, straight-on angle, dramatic spotlight and subtle backlighting, 50mm lens, hyper-realistic textures, ultra-detailed diamonds and fur, luxury magazine aesthetic, surreal scale emphasizing the oversized dog. --ar 2:3 --v 7
```
