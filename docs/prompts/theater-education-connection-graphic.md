## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
Create a bold, modern graphic for a poster that emphasizes connection and togetherness through theater education, while maintaining a sleek, minimalist aesthetic. The central image should feature a group of diverse students in dynamic poses, symbolizing collaboration and emotional growth. Each student should be positioned in an overlapping, interconnected way, showing how theater connects them - whether through shared physical movements, linking arms, or exchanging expressive glances. Use sharp, geometric shapes or lines that subtly connect the figures, giving the design a sense of energy and unity. Include a girl with a headscarf, a boy with dark hair, a person with glasses, and a person in a wheelchair. Make sure all of these students are smiling, joyful, and full of positive energy, conveying happiness and excitement. Their interactions should showcase the playful and expressive nature of theater, highlighting how it brings them together. Use sharp, geometric shapes or lines that subtly connect the figures, giving the design a sense of energy and unity. The background should be simple yet powerful, with abstract forms (such as circles or triangles) representing creativity, growth, and interaction. Incorporate a color palette of bold, contrasting tones like deep navy blue, vibrant orange, and white to create visual impact and a modern feel. Use strong, minimalist typography for the text: "Starke Kinder. Starke Zukunft." (Strong Children. Strong Future.) with a clean, sans-serif font, and the secondary message "Theaterpädagogik in die Schule!" (Theater Education in Schools!) placed beneath in a bold, modern style. The overall design should feel contemporary, fresh, and inspiring, while emphasizing the power of theater education to foster connection and teamwork. keep the original style of the graphic. --ar 2:3 --v 7 --stylize 250
```
