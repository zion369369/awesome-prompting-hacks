## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
Prompt (Stencil-Style Illustration): A powerful, high-contrast stencil-style digital image in bold black, white, and earth tones. A strong Black man plays an electric guitar or vibraphone with passion, his posture grounded like a tree trunk--channeling the element of Earth. Beside him, a radiant Black woman dances with wild grace, her hair flowing upward as if caught in wind--embodying Air. Her dress flares like Fire, with flame-like shapes trailing behind her movements, while swirling waves around her feet represent Water. The background is abstract and cosmic, with minimalist symbols of the four elements surrounding them. Their figures are dynamic and expressive, the stencil lines sharp and energetic, capturing the essence of soul, nature, and ancestral rhythm. Style: stencil/screen-print aesthetic with symbolic overlays, powerful contrast, movement-heavy composition. --v 7
```
