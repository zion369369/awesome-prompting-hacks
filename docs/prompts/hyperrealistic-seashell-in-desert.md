## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
absolutely stunning, hyperrealistic desert scene at golden hour. In the middle of endless golden sand dunes stands an enormous, open seashell so gigantic it resembles a grand museum or ancient monument. Its surface shimmers with soft iridescent colors pearl pinks, silvers, and golds catching the sunlight. Inside the massive shell rests a colossal, glowing pearl, radiating a gentle, magical light that bathes the nearby dunes. Tiny human figures at the base look minuscule in comparison, emphasizing the shell’s breathtaking scale. Fine sand swirls in the warm breeze, the atmosphere feels dreamlike, sacred, and utterly mesmerizing. Ultra-detailed textures, cinematic lighting, hyperrealistic, 32K, awe-inspiring beauty. --ar 9:16 --v 7
```
