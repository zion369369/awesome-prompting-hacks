## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
A young steampunk mechanic’s expression sheet, with grease-smudged cheeks and a tool belt strapped across their chest. The grid highlights contrasting emotions--playful winks, determined scowls, exhausted sighs--each with annotations on jawline tension and eyelid droop. Tiny steam-powered gadgets float around the edges, reacting to the character’s mood shifts. The color palette is muted browns and bronzes, with occasional pops of emerald from glowing dials. --v 7 --stylize 450
```
