## 💡 The Prompt Engineering Code

Copy and paste this structured prompt into your AI console:

```text
This is an advertising poster for shampoo, a bottle of green shampoo for a young beautiful and elegant woman, the photo features a green and light blue minimalist design style. The background is a springtime and light blue lake backdrop. She looks at the product while wearing her hair down, her hair is fluffy and soft and she is wearing a white tank top, no text on or near this beautiful photo captures the freshness and nature --ar 3:4 --v 7
```
